METHODOLOGY
====================================================================================================

This study employs Bayesian inference to model NBA player performance:

1. DATA COLLECTION
   - Season-agnostic data loading with automatic detection
   - Per-game statistics: Points, Rebounds, Assists, Steals, Blocks, Minutes
   - Rolling averages for trend analysis

2. POSTERIOR ESTIMATION
   - Multivariate Gaussian posterior distribution
   - Cold-start priors for missing data
   - Covariance structure captures stat correlations

3. CAPABILITY REGIONS
   - 80% credible ellipsoids in 6-dimensional space
   - Geometric representation: E = {x : (x-mu)^T Sigma^-1 (x-mu) <= chi^2(alpha, d)}
   - Where mu = posterior mean, Sigma = covariance, alpha = 0.80, d = 6
   - Ellipsoid axes determined by eigenvectors of Sigma
   - Axis lengths proportional to sqrt(eigenvalues)
